My name is Handel de la Paz Batista. I consider myself a simple person who always seeks to enjoy life to the fullest. I treat others as I would like to be treated, and I value relationships based on respect and trust.
Furthermore, I love playing video games in my free time, especially Call of Duty or baseball games. Although lately I've been getting hooked on pool games. I also enjoy playing volleyball. I have a calm personality and I tend to be reserved, especially with people I don't know. However, I'm always willing to help in whatever way I can.
Currently, I'm studying Computer Science, and my main goal is to complete my university degree and find a job that allows me to be independent. Although I don't have any notable academic or professional achievements at the moment, I have begun to explore the world of cybersecurity, a field that I find fascinating and full of possibilities. I'm convinced that, if I continue on this path, I'll be able to build a promising future.
I created this website as part of a final project for my class. Through it, I hope to share a little of myself, make my work known, and, perhaps, leave a positive impression on my classmates and professor. Furthermore, I believe that developing these skills will help me communicate effectively with colleagues, superiors, and clients in my future professional career.
